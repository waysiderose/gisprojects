Custom Basemap Using Google

Step 1: Choose an organization.

This exercise operates under customizing a basemap to the colors of an organization. Previously, we had used ArcGIS Esri software to do this. However, what if our client does not have a license?

I chose the non-profit Global Links. They do good work that has local and far reaching impact - for example, they are a primary mask supplier in a volunteer effort I volunteer with.

Step 2: Create a color pallet.

I took a screen shot of the main page of Global Links.

Ran the screenshot through Adobe Color Create's Extract Theme. Tested different themes to get a unique selection of colors that worked together for my reference table.

Created a color pallet reference table with a Column for the Hex Code, Color Name, Sample Image, and RBG Distribution.
